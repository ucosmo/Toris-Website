Hi!

Before anything else, thank you for taking the time to read this. This website actually started as a birthday surprise for you. I wasn't able to finish it in time because of everything I was going through in June, and by the time I was close to finishing it, everything that happened between us had already unfolded. I never got the chance to share it with you. I decided to finish it anyway. Not to change the past or ask anything from you, but because these memories meant a lot to me and I wanted to share them with you. Looking back, I realized it was never the grand moments that stayed with me the most. It was the little conversations, the random stories, the dreams you shared, the late-night talks, and the time we spent together. Those are the memories that have stayed with me ever since. No matter how far apart our paths are today, those memories will always remain a beautiful part of my life. I hope that as you go through these pages, they bring a smile to your face, just as they did to mine while I was putting everything together.

I remember our very first conversation and how everything started. Back then, I never imagined how much that one moment would mean to me. I still think those cookie sweaters are super cute, and somehow they've become one of the little memories that always makes me smile.

What we had during uni times has been such a rollercoaster ride. We made so many memories together. We talked about our struggles, shared our stories, did a lot of activities together, laughed over the smallest stuffs, played countless games together, slowly got to know each other more and more, and our late night text & calls. I always loved hearing your dreams, like me usually appearing in your dreams, going to the wildest adventures, have crazy plot twists, and so much more. Even the little details always make me smile. I'll always be grateful and never forget those times.

I will never forget the times you've been going through at life, the stories you've always shared at work. I'll always look forward to what happens next.

I've always admired how hardworking and dedicated you are. No matter how busy or exhausting things became, you always gave your best. I'm truly proud of everything you've accomplished, and I hope you continue working hard and achieving all the goals you've set for yourself. I'm genuinely happy for you.

One Last Thing

Looking back, I realize that I never truly let you understand why I struggled so much during our last conversation. From your perspective, it probably looked like I simply chose them over you, and I understand why that hurt so much.

The truth is more complicated than I ever managed to explain. It wasn't because you weren't enough or because I didn't care. It came from years of things I had been carrying long before we ever met, and when everything happened, I didn't know how to put those feelings into words. I was scared that no matter how I explained it, it would only make things worse.

I'm not writing this to change your mind or ask you to reconsider anything. I just don't want someone who meant so much to me to remember me through an incomplete story. There were reasons behind my silence that I never found the courage to explain, and I hope that, at the very least, you know my feelings for you were always genuine.
